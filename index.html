How to Play

Check the power box, then click start. The computer will start the round by lighting up a color. The player clicks the color and the computer will move to the second round where it will consecutively add more colors in sequence. The player must match the sequence. If you do not check "strict" and you miss a color, the round will restart. In strict mode, if you miss a color then the game will restart from the beginning. You win if you complete 15 rounds.
